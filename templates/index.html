About us...

VeganCaju is a vegan truffle store. Created with the goal to provide a quality chocolate to our customers, without the need to harm animals. Thr truffles are 100% vegan and handmade, made out cocconut milk. All prepared with all the love and care in the world.

How does it work?

First, you have to make and order. You can choose between diferent box sizes and flavors and when you want to pick it up. Once the order is made, it is going to have a "waiting for approval" status. After your order is confirmed by our team, your order will appear as "Accepted" and you can pick it up on the choosen date. You can only cancel or change order before it's confirmed.
