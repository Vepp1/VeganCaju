About us...

VeganCaju is a vegan truffle store. Created with the goal to provide quality chocolate to our customers, without the need to harm animals. The truffles are 100% vegan and handmade, made out of coconut milk. All prepared with all the love and care in the world.

How does it work?

First, you have to make and order. You can choose between different box sizes and flavors and when you want to pick it up. Once the order is made, it is going to have a "waiting for approval" status. After your order is confirmed by our team, your order will appear as "Accepted" and you can pick it up on the chosen date. You can only cancel or change the order before it's confirmed.
